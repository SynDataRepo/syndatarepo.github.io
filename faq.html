What is synthetic data?

Synthetic data is carefully created data, based on real datasets, which retain the statistical properties and correlations of their real data counterparts while having no connection to real data subjects. This means it is free from data protection regulations and can be used, shared and traded openly. Collecting, storing, handling and protecting certain categories of data is cumbersome, expensive and means risk exposure. Synthetic data removes these restrictions on data use.
